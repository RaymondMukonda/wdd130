History

Splash White Water Rafting in Perthshire, Scotland was founded in the early 2000s. The company started as a small operation with the goal of making white water rafting accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

Our team is passionate about creating unforgettable experiences on the water. We offer thrilling outdoor ventures for everyone, from first-timers to seasoned rafters. Our mission is to provide safe, exciting, and memorable rafting trips that connect people with nature and each other.
